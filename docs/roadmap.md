# Product Roadmap

## Year 1: Foundation
* Add multiple bank account support.
* Mobile-responsive layout improvements.
* Goal tracking (e.g., "Save $5000 for vacation").

## Year 3: Advanced Intelligence
* **Receipt Scanning:** OCR integration using Tesseract to scan paper receipts.
* **Local LLM Chat:** Integrate `llama.cpp` to ask "How much did I spend on sushi last year?" in natural language.
* **Recurring Bill Detection:** Auto-detect subscriptions.

## Year 5: Financial Twin
* Full "Financial Digital Twin" simulation.
* Market simulation integration to project investment growth alongside budget.

## Year 10: Legacy
* Decentralized data storage (optional IPFS backup).
* Complete autonomy (auto-fetching from banks via open banking APIs if free tiers exist).
